# EmbeddingModelComparison

Inline figure for Chapter 2 — Embeddings.

Displays a table-of-bars comparing 6 real production embedding models across three dimensions: vector dimensionality, cost per 1M tokens, and MTEB benchmark score. Each row is color-coded by vendor. Bars for cost and MTEB score are relative; the MTEB axis is zoomed to the 62–65 range where real models cluster.

Teaches: how models compare on the quality-cost frontier, and why leaderboard rank often matters less than operational fit.
