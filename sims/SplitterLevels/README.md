# SplitterLevels

Interactive radio-button selector across Kamradt's 5 levels of text-splitter sophistication, applied to a concrete refund policy paragraph. Shows chunk boundaries via colored left-border highlights on each chunk span.

## Interactions
- Click any of the 5 level buttons to see how that splitter carves the source text
- Stats line below shows chunk count, max chunk length, and semantic alignment indicator

## Placement
After the five-levels table in the "The five levels of text splitting" sub-section of Chapter 1.
